Our Story

HUDUMA YA UDIAKONIA

Kuhusu Udiakonia

Udiakonia ni huduma ya upendo inayotolewa na kanisa au waumini kwa watu walioko katika hali ya uhitaji, kama maskini, wagonjwa, yatima, wajane na waliokata tamaa, kwa lengo la kuonyesha upendo wa Kristo kwa vitendo.

Our Mission

To serve God by serving others through acts of love, justice, and compassion—responding to human need, defending the dignity of all people, and working towards healing, empowerment, and transformation in individuals and communities.

Our Vision

To see a compassionate and just society where the love of Christ is expressed through practical service, and where every person — especially the vulnerable — is treated with dignity, supported in their needs, and empowered to live with hope and purpose.

Our Programs

Children Education

Helping children in education by ensuring that those who drop out return to school and providing them with school needs.

Women Empowerment

Making efforts to connect homeless children with their families to get the best care and close attention, or shelter if reunification fails.

Rehabilitation Counseling

Helping children who are addicted to drugs, disabled, or treated with acts of violence, to build them mentally to be able to live their dreams.

Health Services

Providing health insurance services to street children who cannot afford medical and food expenses because of poverty.

Food Program

Providing food and clean water to starving kids and kids living in centers. Food for children is very important.

Diakonia Outreach

In cases where reunification of street children with their families fails, we take responsibility of sheltering street kids with homes or centers and clothing.
